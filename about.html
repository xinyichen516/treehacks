Eliminating the Shelter

Finding safe, reliable homes for pets in your community.

Why We Want to Eliminate the Shelter
People often find themselves surrendering their pets to animal shelters due to financial circumstances, relocation, or behavioral issues - in hopes of the pet finding a better future. However, due to overcrowding and underfunding at these shelters, pets are cramped into small rooms, and thousands of healthy dogs and cats are euthanized in these shelters.

This is why we want to reduce the number of pets entering these shelters. We want to make adopting and fostering a pet to be a fair and easy process. We want you to participate in home-to-home adoption, rather than home-to-shelter-to-home adoption.
